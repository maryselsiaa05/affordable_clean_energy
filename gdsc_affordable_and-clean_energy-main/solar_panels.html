Solar Panels (Photovoltaic Cells)
  Solar panels, made from photovoltaic (PV) cells, are designed to convert sunlight into electricity. These are the core component of most solar installations and come in various types:


     Monocrystalline Panels: Known for higher efficiency and a sleeker appearance, these are ideal for small roof spaces. Polycrystalline Panels: More affordable than monocrystalline, though slightly less efficient. Thin-Film Panels: Lightweight and flexible, suitable for roofs that cannot support heavier panels.
